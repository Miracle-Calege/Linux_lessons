mkdir->usado para criar directorios

mkdir+dir->cria um directorio

ex:mkdir image

mkdir+dir.pai/sub.dir->cria um subdirectorio no directorio image

ex:mkdir image/pics

mkdir -p dir/sub.dir->cria simultaneamente o directorio e o subdirectorio

ex: mkdir -p names/mark

mkdir -p dir/{subdir1,subdir2,subdir3}->para criar um directorio e varios subdirectorios

ex:mkdir -p names/mark/{dir1,dir2,dir3}
